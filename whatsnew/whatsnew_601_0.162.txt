0.162 2015/05/27 [#601 - Miodrag Milanović]

As of 0.162 the MAME and MESS projects have been combined into a single emulator. The changes listed below cover both. MAME is in the process of becoming a Free and Open Source project. If you have contributed code and we have not contacted you yet regarding this license change, or if you believe you are miscredited in the source code, please
contact us.
